Who we are

Zero2Billion, founded by Prakadesh Subramanian, a veteran in life sciences and engineering, stands as India's trailblazing private incubation hub. Operating as a Section 8 entity, we've fostered over 25+ agri-startups, 45+ tech startups, and 5+ enterprises, solidifying our stature as an agri-tech incubation powerhouse.

Our goal

Empowering idea generators and budding talents, shepherding them from concept to market penetration. Committed to nurturing a sustainable entrepreneurial landscape, we leverage strategic metrics to uplift institutions and assert our dominance in the sector. Join us in our fervent quest to cultivate entrepreneurs and fuel India's trillion-dollar economy.
