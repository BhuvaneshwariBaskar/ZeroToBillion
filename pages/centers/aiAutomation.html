Centre for AI, Embedded & Automation

Embedded Systems, comprising hardware and software components tightly integrated into devices and machinery, are the backbone of numerous applications. AI algorithms are now being embedded directly into these systems, enabling them to analyze data, make decisions, and adapt in real-time

Features

  • The integration of AI into embedded systems empowers devices to perceive, reason, and act autonomously, enhancing their decision-making capabilities and adaptability.
  • Embedded systems equipped with AI algorithms can process vast amounts of data in real-time, enabling timely responses and actions without reliance on external computing resources.
  • Efficient AI algorithms tailored for embedded systems prioritize low power consumption, crucial for battery-operated devices and IoT applications, ensuring prolonged operation and enhanced energy efficiency.
  • Embedded systems leverage sensors and perception technologies to gather data from the surrounding environment, enabling AI algorithms to analyze and interpret information for context-aware decision-making.

Services

  • Service providers offer custom development of embedded AI solutions tailored to specific applications, including algorithm design, hardware selection, software development, and integration.
  • Companies specialize in integrating AI-powered embedded systems into existing infrastructure, ensuring compatibility, reliability, and seamless operation across diverse environments.
  • Service providers optimize embedded AI solutions for performance, power consumption, and resource utilization, employing techniques such as algorithmic optimization, hardware acceleration, and power management.
  • Services encompass security assessment, encryption, and compliance with industry standards and regulations to safeguard embedded AI systems against cyber threats and ensure data privacy and integrity.
