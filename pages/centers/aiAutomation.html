Centre for AI, Embedded & Automation

We're at the forefront of technological innovation. Leveraging our extensive experience and expertise, we provide invaluable support to startups in navigating the dynamic fields of AI, embedded systems, and automation. Through workshops, hands-on training, and industry-level projects, we equip entrepreneurs with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in these cutting-edge industries.

Here's how we empower innovation:

  • Technical Training: Expert-led workshops and hands-on training in AI, embedded systems, and automation.
  • Industry Projects: Opportunities for startups to engage in industry-level projects for practical experience.
  • Consultation Services: Tailored guidance and support to address specific challenges in AI, embedded systems, and automation.
  • Funding Facilitation: Assistance in securing funding and navigating financial challenges.
  • Community Connections: Access to a supportive network of industry experts and fellow entrepreneurs for collaboration and knowledge exchange.


Fostering Sustainable Progress for Individuals, Institutions, and Industries!
